Aithyuia
(αἴθυια)

A poetic word, of uncertain or indefinite meaning. Probably a large Gull, e.g. Larus Marinus, the Black-backed Gull(Sundevall), or Larus Argentatus, the Herring Gull (Krüper), the former being rare in Greece. Νetolickaʼs hypothesis of the Merganser, and that of Groshans that it was a Diver or Grebe, do not tally with Αristotle : Schneiderʼs identification with the Skua, Lestris Parasiticus, fails, inasmuch as the latter does not dive (vide Buchholz,op.cit. pp. 112, 113) nor does it breed in the Mediterranean. The Herring Gull is abundant during the winter and breeds about the middle of April: the Common Tern (Sterna anglica) lays about the same time (Krüper) but in the lagoons and not on the cliffs.
